Featuring over a dozen devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, computers, a smart TV and wearables from Apple, Android, Kindle and Windows, the device wall instantly populates all devices with your website or application to facilitate testing.

There is no better way to test for user experience than on the user's actual device.
